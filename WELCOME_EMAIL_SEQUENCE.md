## Technical setup notes

### Email platform options

1. **Beehiiv** (already in the PRINTMAXX stack): Free tier supports sequences. Set up as an automation triggered by "community signup" tag.
2. **ConvertKit/Kit**: Free up to 1,000 subscribers. Visual automation builder for sequences.
3. **Buttondown**: Simple, developer-friendly. $9/mo for sequences.

### Trigger setup

- **Discord:** When someone joins the server, MEE6 or a custom bot captures their email (via a Google Form in #start-here) and adds them to the email list with "community_discord" tag.
- **Skool:** Skool captures email on signup automatically. Export weekly and add to email list with "community_skool" tag.
- **Telegram:** Use Whop or a custom bot to capture email during payment. Add to list with "community_telegram" tag.
- **All platforms:** Merge into one sequence. The emails are platform-agnostic.

### Segmentation

Tag subscribers by:
- `source`: discord, skool, telegram, twitter, reddit
- `tier`: free, paid_basic, paid_pro
- `challenge_status`: not_started, in_progress, completed
- `days_since_join`: for time-based triggers

### Metrics to track

| Metric | Target | Action if below |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Open rate | > 40% | Test subject lines, adjust send times |
| Click rate | > 8% | Improve CTAs, test link placement |
| Reply rate | > 3% | Add more "reply to this" prompts |
| Unsubscribe rate | < 2% per email | Reduce frequency or improve value |
| Paid conversion (Email 5) | > 5% | Test pricing, improve social proof |

### A/B test plan

- Email 1: Test 3 subject lines over first 300 subscribers
- Email 5: Test "$29/mo" framing vs. "$0.96/day" framing
- All emails: Test send time (8 AM vs. 10 AM vs. 6 PM in subscriber's timezone)

## Sequence timing rationale

| Gap | Why |
|-----|-----|
| Day 0 (immediate) | Strike while they're interested. Deliver value before they forget they signed up. |
| Day 2 | Give them time to explore, then show them what they missed. |
| Day 5 | Social proof hits after they've had time to see the community. Creates belonging. |
| Day 7 | Challenge after a full week. They know the community. Now it's time to act. |
| Day 10 | Upgrade pitch after they've experienced the full free value. They know what they're buying. |

---

## Post-sequence

After the 5-email sequence completes:

- Move subscriber to "weekly digest" list (1 email/week, best-of from the community)
- If they upgraded: move to "paid member" list (weekly paid-only newsletter with advanced tactics)
- If they didn't upgrade: send 1 more soft pitch at Day 21, then stop pitching. Keep them on the weekly digest. Some people convert after 30-60 days.
- Re-engage at Day 30 with: "you've been a member for 30 days. here's what you missed this month [top 5 alpha drops]."
