## Discovery network optimization (ranking on Skool)

Skool ranks communities by:
1. **Activity score** (daily posts, comments, reactions)
2. **Member count** (more members = higher rank)
3. **Retention** (members who stay > members who churn)
4. **Category relevance** (keyword matching)

### How to rank

1. **Category:** Select "Business" > "Entrepreneurship" or "Marketing" (test which has less competition)
2. **Keywords in name + description:** "solopreneur," "indie hacker," "solo business," "make money online," "side project"
3. **Daily posting cadence:** Never skip a day. Skool rewards consistency above all.
4. **Engagement farming:** Ask questions in every post. Polls. Challenges. Make it impossible to just read without replying.
5. **Cross-promote:** Every Twitter post, every Reddit comment, every email should have a Skool link.
6. **Member milestones:** Celebrate publicly. "We hit 100 members" posts get shared by members on their own socials.
7. **Free tier generosity:** The more value in the free tier, the more members join, the higher you rank, the more paid conversions.

### Category keywords to include in descriptions

"solopreneur community," "indie hacker," "make money online," "solo business," "side hustle to full time," "digital products," "freelance," "cold email," "content creation," "app building"
